Zero Waste Lifestyle is Now Hype! Are You Ready, Kids?

Zero waste LIFESTYLE never enough to discuss about. As we know that healthy lifestyle is recently interesting for millenials since some of millenial’s thoughts it just so damn cool if someone have ‘zero waste lifestyle’ and so do I. Here is some tips everyone can try to start Zero Waste Lifestyle;

  1. Reduce everything we consume and try to reduce our daily waste production.  
  2. Reusing everything we have until it no longer works. For example, try to bring shopping bag from canvas rather than buy plastic bag from supermarket. 
  3. Recycling things that possible, like organic or compostable items become secondhand thing that valuable.
  4. Refuse what we do not need. For example, bring your own utensils whenever we go to restaurant or canteen and buy food, try not to accept plastic straw, plastic spoon and other plastic utensils. Well, try to say no to everything that’s not essential in daily life.
  5. Repairing what we can. Everyone seems like have a broken things, like mobile phone, clothes, and others. Try to repair it rather than buy a new one will help reducing waste for sure.

 

The five things above is not only for plastic waste, but can also be used for various other things. We can conclude that the beginning of zero waste life style is step on hard thing first since it takes time to change our habit. Some city in Indonesia is now start have a zero waste store and all of the items just so awesome. They provide everything we need in zero waste life style, for example like bamboo toothbrush, bamboo straw, shopping bag, menspad, organic cup for menstruation, and other awesome thing that will help to save our planet. Try to calculate before and after you doing zero waste lifestyle and you will find out that it also save money for sure!
