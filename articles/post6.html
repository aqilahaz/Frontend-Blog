Become A Volunteer in Beach Clean Up Day, Why Not?

Have you ever imagine, one day you will sink below a huge plastic waste spread in a beach? Well, it may be happen in our grandhild’s era. The problems of plastic waste issues is getting worst day by day. Fortunately, there is an organization that arrange an action to clean up the beach. International Coastal Cleanup Day 2018, which took place on September 15 and has united small groups in various parts of the world to clean up the trash on the beach. It is said that this activity was started over 30 years ago begin with just two individuals try to clean up the beaches by removing trash. Who ever guess that it will turned into a true global movement in nowadays era?


Since Plastic Oceans Organization said that at least tens of thousands of volunteers and hundreds of organizations was participating in Coastal Cleanup Day 2018.



Cleanup the beach will be a great idea, since we know that true power to tackle plastic waste problems is doing a real action, rather than just play in the beach and doing selfie with friends then upload it in social media. Well, how about clean up the beach with your friends, and ask more friends to do the same thing if they were go to beach! Can you imagine, hundred of persons comes to work together cleanup the beach in your country at least once in a month. The imagination of play in the beach and sink below the huge plastic waste will never happen, right? We can call it as ‘Join Clean Up The Beach Campaign and You Will Be Cool Guy’. Well, I’m pretty sure you will get more likes on your social media.
