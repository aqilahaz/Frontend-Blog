Goodbye Synthetic Plastic! Welcome, Seaweed-based Packaging Era!

Recently, there are so many influencer make campaign about financially dependent by make a start-up company. We do believe that by make our own start-up company, we can earn money with easy way; based on our passion, get the sources nearby, and increasing the value of a product respectively. For example, if you are one of young people that have awareness in plastic waste problems, you may get inspires by Evoware company. Evoware is social enterprise, based on seaweed as resources and turn it become bioplastic that has high economic value.



Well, Evoware is a unique real life action that can reduce plastic waste amount in nature. FYI, if people use Evoware's products as their daily packaging, people sure evolve to be closer to nature and live a more responsible and sustainable life. Evoware believe that they here to be the eco-solution for plastic waste problems in Indonesia country. As everyone know that Indonesia is the largest archipelago in the world and two-thirds of its territory is water and Indonesia is one of the world’s largest seaweed producers and have high potency to produce bioplastic from seaweed since the impact is not just for environment, but also the livelihood of seaweed farmers in Indonesia.
